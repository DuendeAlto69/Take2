Our Story from the beginning to now

"I first saw her on a zoom call and I thought to myself this has to be the prettiest girl i've ever seen! I couldn't take mt eyes off of her, I thought to myself "Why have I never seen her before" (I had lived in this ward for my whole life and I had never seen her before). "She must be new to the ward" I thought, but sadly this was covid times so I had no way of meeting her in person or to get her number. So I had to wait it out until I finally laid eyes on her in person. We had an in person sunday school class at the park and she was there. Being the courageous person I was I didn't saya t hing to her. I didn't even speak a word to her until one fateful day when we had a activity at a local pool and we both happened to go. I was being a boy and I was playing a keepaway game with a ball and a long throw went right to her and I didn't even know her name but I asked for the ball at the same time a leader she knew asked for it. I was very lucky and she handed me the ball as the leader said "Ohhhhh I see how it is" I smirked happily knowing I wanted to really meet her. Then the first time I really talked with her was when I went to a couple day youth camp where we hit it off. I hoped I wasn't being too obvious as I was practically glued to her any chance I had. There was one fateful night where we just stayed up all night talking by a fire and laughing and that's when I first knew that I loved this girl. There was just one major problem, I was about to leave on an LDS mission and didn't have very much time to spend with her. I did my best and I got her number and we talked and talked all the time even when I was in the home MTC. Then I left but she continued to email me every single week. Until one day the emails stopped. I thought to myself "Oh no, she got a boyfriend" I hoped it wasn't true but the emails grew scarse and then finally I got an email a few months later saying she had broken up with her boyfriend as she proceeded to tell me that she would be waiting for me and I got excited. We grew really close over those two years even though we never saw each other in person. The first week I was back I went on a date with her and it was like I had never even left. Things got serious very quickly within a couple of weeks we were offically dating and shortly after we said the powerful words "I love you". Then just four months after I had returned home we were enagaged. Time has flown very quickly since that day. We now are only 10 short days away from being married. I am so excited to marry the love of my life and my best friend. I love her more than I can descibe with words and I know that she loves me too. She has made me the happiest man to ever walk this earth and I will treat her the way she deserves it for the rest of time and eternity." -Brodie
